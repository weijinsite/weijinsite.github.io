I am an Associate Professor at the Center for Transnational Studies and the Institute of International Economics, School of Economics, Nankai University.

My research fields are international economics, international trade, and international business.
 
### Education

- Ph.D. in Economics, Singapore Management University, Singapore 2013–2018
- M.S. in Economics, University of Macau, Macau 2011–2013
- B.A. in Management, Central University of Finance and Economics, Beijing 2007–2011
